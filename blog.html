The Engineering School at Chitkara University has gone from strength to strength since its inception in 2002. Aside from being rated as the best engineering college in Punjab, the engineering programs at Chitkara have consistently figured in the list of top 50 programs in the country. A perfect blend of academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and proactive industry tie-ups are what lies behind Chitkara’s stellar results. If you have a technical bent of mind and are keen on solving the world’s myriad problems by designing innovative technical solutions, then enrolling in one of the engineering programs at Chitkara University is an excellent idea. Graduating from Chitkara will ensure a bright future and an extremely satisfying career.
